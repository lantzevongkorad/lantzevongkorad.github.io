Moderate Reinhardt Cardinals and Constructible Push-Downs

By Anonymous Kid • April 7, 2025

Abstract: We study moderately Reinhardt cardinals in models of MK without AC and demonstrate a method of constructible push-down. The proof avoids V = L while preserving large cardinal consistency. Definitions of “moderate” Reinhardt cardinals are used to generalize embeddings in an inner model framework. We further discuss absoluteness and the role of definability under modified KP assumptions.
